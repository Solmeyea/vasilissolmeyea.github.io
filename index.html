Solmeyea is a hybrid AgTech company intersecting with CleanTech, BioTech & Agriculture. Our aim is to act supplementary and welcome overpopulation. We simultaneously tackle climate change & humanity’s nutritional needs by producing high value algal bio-based products & proteins for food, feed & pharm


Our Mission


We aim to make sure the rapidly growing population can get fed & cured sustainably, without further damaging the environment and by combining our top-notch technologies.


Technology


Our technology is based on CO2 utilization through vertical microalgae cultivation. We do so, in a controlled closed system ensuring optimal sunlight, land and water use.
